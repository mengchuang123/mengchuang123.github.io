第一篇文章是关于U-net模型结合多模态医学图像做图像分割的研究。文章的任务是多模态图像分割。主要的思路是采用图像加权来处理多模态数据。同时不同模态图像的权重是可以被训练的。考虑到图像分割的区域病灶较小，而且集中在某一个部位，研究还引入注意力机制来提高分割性能。这个是我第一次做深度学习相关的科研项目，给了我许多启发和经验。

第二篇文章是采用半监督学习的方式来做小样本医学图像分割。在我们的数据集中，无标签数据占了90%以上，文章的目的是充分利用这些无标签数据提高分割精度。研究的思路是引入一个重建任务作为分割任务的正则项，使得任务变成一个半监督的任务。

我尝试做自己做这个研究，写了一篇文章。学长帮我修改了这篇文章。这个经历让我对计算机视觉的科学研究有了更深的认识。

The first article is about the research of U-net model combining multi-modal medical image to do image segmentation. The task of this paper is multimodal image segmentation. The main idea is to use image weighting to process multi-modal data. At the same time, the weights of different modal images can be trained. Considering that the focus of image segmentation issmall and concentrated in a certain region, attention mechanism is introduced to improve the segmentation performance. This is my first time to do a research project related to deep learning, which has given me a lot of inspiration and experience.

The second article is to use semi-supervised learning to do small sample medical image segmentation. In our dataset, unlabeled data accounts for more than 90%, and the purpose of this article is to make full use of these unlabeled data to improve segmentation accuracy. The idea of the research is to introduce a reconstruction task as a regular term for thesegmentation task, making the task a semi-supervised task.

I tried doing this research myself and wrote an article. The senior helped me revise this article. This experience gave me a deeper understanding of scientific research in computer vision.

7.体系结构和计算机组成的区别

体系结构是抽象的，计算机组成是具体的实现，包括硬件实现等。

体系结构是程序员眼中的计算机，可能包括指令集、数据类型、寻址方式等

一个计算机是否支持乘法指令是体系结构的问题，用什么硬件实现乘法指令是组成的问题。

concrete and abstract

The architecture is abstract, and the computer composition is the concrete realization, including hardware realization and so on.

Architecture is the computer in the eyes of programmers, which may include instruction set, data type, addressing mode, etc. Whether a computer supports multiplication instructions is a problem of architecture, and what hardware is used to implement multiplication instructions is a problem of composition.
